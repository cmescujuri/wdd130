Overview

Purpose

To provide accessible information about Dry Oar white water rafting. This information will include services provided, contact information, appointment scheduling, safety precautions, and our commitment to creating a exciting and memorable experience.

Audience

Ages 12+ that come from financially secure households with a healthy lifestyle looking for a exciting, physically active outdoor experience on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers. Site accessible through desktop, laptop, or mobile devices.
