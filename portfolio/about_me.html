About Me

Cass Escujuri

Web Developer, Student

BYU-Idaho

Hello! I was born in 1994 and grew up moving to different states quite a bit due to my father's love for flipping homes. After college, I was a medical assistant for several years before switching my career to become a web developer. If the opportunities arise, I would love to work for an online medical electronic records company to help create more efficient and user-friendly experiences for workers in the medical field. Many healthcare professionals are from older generations that do not understand technology to the extent that the developers do. Having worked with those older generations, I plan to bring their perspectives to my future developer coworkers so that their concerns can be heard.
