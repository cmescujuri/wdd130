Portfolio

Those that will come to my site will be future employers interested in seeing my online portfolio containing projects I have completed. It will answer the question of what sort of skill and creativity I have in various programming languages - which are currently limited to HTML, CSS, and Python - and where I plan to grow those languages in the future through continuing education. JavaScript is a language I have no knowledge of experience using that will be valuable as a web developer, but a class in the future will help me learn to put this language to use. My current certificate will prepare me to work as a front-end developer, with future certificates preparing me to also work as a back-end developer. This will give me the necessary resources to enter the career field as a junior web development intern, with the hopes of someday becoming a senior full-stack web developer.
